檔案路徑

project/
└── src/
    └── css/
        ├── all.scss
        ├── helpers/
        │   └── _variable.scss
        └── component/
            └── _sidebar.scss

  1. .src(‘src/css/**/*.scss’) 獲取all.scss、_variable.scss、_sidebar.scss

  1. .src(‘src/css//.scss’) 獲取_variable.scss、_sidebar.scss

  1. .src(‘src/css/*.scss’) 獲取all.scss

  1. 下載aos並解壓

  1. 將<head>加入<link href="https://unpkg.com/aos@2.3.1/dist/aos.css" rel="stylesheet"/>

  1. 將<body>加入<script src="https://unpkg.com/aos@2.3.1/dist/aos.js"><script>

  1. 將<body>加入<script>AOS.init()<script>
